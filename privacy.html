1 Introduction

Role Garden LLC ("Role Garden," "we," "us," "our") operates the Role Garden career development platform available at rolegarden.com and app.rolegarden.com (collectively, the "Service"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information.

This Privacy Policy is incorporated by reference into our Terms of Service. By creating an account or using the Service, you agree to the practices described in this Policy.

Questions? Contact us at support@rolegarden.com.

2 Information We Collect
2.1 Information You Provide Directly
  • Account information: email address, name, and password (or OAuth credentials via Google or LinkedIn);
  • Career Data: resume, work history, education, skills, career preferences, target roles, target industries, target companies, and location preferences;
  • Generated Content: cover letters, outreach messages, interview preparation materials, and other content you create through the Service;
  • Payment information: billing name and payment card details, processed by Stripe, Inc. (we do not store your full card number);
  • Communications: messages you send us via email or support channels.
2.2 Information We Collect Automatically
  • Usage data: pages visited, features used, search queries, clicks, and time spent;
  • Device and browser information: IP address, browser type and version, operating system, and device identifiers;
  • Analytics and conversion events: tracked via Google Analytics 4 and Google Tag Manager. Ad measurement pixels (Meta Pixel, Google Ads) are triggered only on specific account creation and subscription conversion events within the Service, not on general page views. See Section 7 for full details;
  • Session data: authentication tokens stored in your browser via Supabase Auth.
2.3 Information from Third Parties
  • OAuth providers: if you sign in with Google or LinkedIn, we receive your name, email address, and profile information from those providers;
  • Job listing data: we source opportunity data from employer career pages and ATS providers (Workday, Greenhouse, Lever, Ashby) to power matching;
  • Payment processor: Stripe provides payment confirmation and subscription status information.
3 How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain the Service, including matching your Career Data to job opportunities and generating personalized content;
  • Process your Career Data through third-party AI services (currently Anthropic's Claude API) for job matching, scoring, resume analysis, cover letter generation, and interview preparation;
  • Process payments and manage your subscription through Stripe;
  • Send transactional emails (account confirmations, daily match notifications, billing notices) via Resend;
  • Send marketing emails about Role Garden features and updates via Klaviyo (you may unsubscribe at any time);
  • Measure the performance of advertising campaigns via Meta Pixel and Google Ads conversion tracking, triggered only on account creation and subscription activation events — not on general page views;
  • Analyze aggregate usage patterns to improve the Service via Google Analytics 4;
  • Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and security incidents;
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms of Service.

5 AI Processing and Anthropic
  • Your Career Data (including resume content) is transmitted to Anthropic's servers for processing. As of the effective date of this Policy, Anthropic states it does not use API customer content to train its foundation models. We will notify you of material changes to this.
  • Processed data may temporarily reside on Anthropic's infrastructure per their standard service operation practices.
  • AI-generated outputs are returned to Role Garden and stored in your account on our infrastructure.
  • We may change AI processing providers in the future and will update this Policy accordingly.

By creating an account, you consent to this AI processing as described in Section 7 of our Terms of Service.

6 Data Retention
  • Account and Career Data: retained for the life of your account, plus up to 30 days after deletion;
  • Payment records: retained as required by applicable law, typically 7 years;
  • Usage logs and analytics: retained in aggregate or anonymized form for up to 24 months;
  • Communications: retained up to 3 years for support and dispute resolution;
  • Backup copies: may persist in encrypted backups for up to 90 days after active deletion.

You may request deletion of your account and Career Data at any time by contacting support@rolegarden.com. We will process requests within 30 days.

7 Cookies, Tracking, and Ad Measurement
7.1 Our Approach

Role Garden uses a conversion-event approach to ad measurement rather than general behavioral tracking. This means:

  • Ad measurement pixels (Meta Pixel, Google Ads) fire only on specific, high-intent events — account creation and subscription activation — for users who have agreed to our Terms of Service. They do not fire on general page views or for anonymous visitors.
  • By creating an account and agreeing to our Terms of Service, you consent to this conversion-event tracking as part of our service operations.
  • Google Analytics 4 is used to measure aggregate usage patterns within the Service. GA4 data is associated with your account and subject to Google's privacy policies.
7.2 Cookie Types

Essential cookies (cannot be declined — required for the Service to function):

  • Supabase Auth session token — maintains your authenticated session.

Analytics cookies (first-party, covered by Terms of Service acceptance):

  • Google Analytics 4 — tracks aggregate usage patterns and Service performance.

Conversion measurement (event-based, covered by Terms of Service acceptance):

  • Meta Pixel — fires on account creation and subscription activation events only.
  • Google Ads conversion tags — fire on account creation and subscription activation events only.
7.3 Local Storage

Within the app, we use browser local storage (not cookies) to cache your career profile, search results, and session preferences. This is essential to app performance.

7.4 Opting Out

You may opt out of Google Analytics tracking at any time using the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on. To opt out of ad measurement tracking entirely, contact support@rolegarden.com or delete your account.

8.4 California Residents (CCPA/CPRA)

If you are a California resident, you have the following rights:

  • Right to Know: request information about the categories and specific pieces of personal information we collect, use, and disclose;
  • Right to Delete: request deletion of personal information, subject to certain exceptions;
  • Right to Correct: request correction of inaccurate personal information;
  • Right to Opt Out of Sale or Sharing: Role Garden does not sell personal information. Conversion-event tracking via Meta and Google may constitute "sharing" for cross-context behavioral advertising under CCPA. You may opt out by contacting support@rolegarden.com;
  • Right to Limit Use of Sensitive Personal Information: to the extent your Career Data constitutes sensitive personal information under CPRA, you may request that we limit its use to what is necessary to provide the Service;
  • Right to Non-Discrimination: we will not discriminate against you for exercising your CCPA/CPRA rights.

To exercise your California rights, contact support@rolegarden.com. We will respond within 45 days.

8.5 Other State Residents

Residents of Virginia, Colorado, Connecticut, Utah, Texas, Oregon, and other states with comprehensive privacy laws have similar rights. Contact support@rolegarden.com to exercise them.

9 Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures including:

  • Row-Level Security (RLS) on all Supabase database tables — users can only access their own data;
  • Encrypted data transmission via HTTPS/TLS for all communications;
  • Authentication via Supabase Auth with industry-standard session management;
  • Payment data processed by Stripe using PCI-DSS compliant infrastructure — we never store your full card number.

No method of electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your information.
